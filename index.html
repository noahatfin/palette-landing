What is Palette

Every story deserves
to be seen.
Now every story
can be filmed.

Palette is the world's first AI-native short film studio. We give every storyteller — not just those with cameras, crews, or budgets — the power to turn ideas into cinematic video.

Our models understand narrative structure, visual language, pacing, and emotion. They don't just generate clips — they craft stories.

From a single sentence to a fully produced short film. In minutes.

What Palette does

01
Script AI

Write your idea in plain language. Palette's narrative AI structures it into a scene-by-scene script complete with dialogue, pacing, and emotional beats.

02
AI Studio Director

Our director model decides shot composition, color grade, sound design, and visual style — adapting to your genre automatically.

03
4K Export

Render your film in broadcast-quality 4K with licensed music, color grading, and captions ready for TikTok, Instagram, or film festivals.

Process

Three steps.
One masterpiece.

Step 01

Describe your vision

Type your idea. A genre, a mood, a scene — even a single sentence. Palette's AI understands context and intent.

Step 02

AI produces your film

Script, storyboard, visual generation, music composition — everything happens automatically in under 3 minutes.

Step 03

Share or export

Post directly to social media or download your 4K film. Edit in-platform or bring it into your workflow.
